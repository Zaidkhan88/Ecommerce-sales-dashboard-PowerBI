# E-Commerce Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and payment trends.

The dashboard enables business users to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and gain actionable insights for data-driven decision-making.

---



## Business Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify top-performing customers and products
- Evaluate category-wise and sub-category profitability
- Understand customer purchasing behavior
- Analyze payment mode preferences
- Track monthly profit trends

## Key Insights

- Maharashtra generated the highest sales revenue.
- Clothing contributed the largest share of quantity sold.
- COD emerged as the most preferred payment method.
- Printers and Sarees were among the most profitable sub-categories.
- Sales and profit performance varied significantly across quarters.
